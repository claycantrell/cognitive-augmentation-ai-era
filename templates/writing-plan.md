# Writing Plan — Section-by-Section Drafting Skeleton

**Target length:** ~_____ words (_____ – _____ range)

**Structural spine:** Identify the paper's central distinction or throughline. Name it here. It should appear in the introduction, anchor the conceptual center, recur through the main sections, and close the paper.

**Drafting rule:** Each section should do only one argumentative job. If a section is trying to do two or three things at once, split the jobs or cut the extras.

**Tone rule:** Stay precise, controlled, and slightly cooler than the underlying idea. Note which sections are most at risk of sprawl or tonal drift.
