# Cognitive Augmentation in the AI Era

A scientific publication exploring the necessity of pharmaceutical and cognitive modification technologies for human relevance and survival in the face of accelerating artificial intelligence.

## Thesis

As AI systems rapidly surpass human cognitive capabilities, pharmaceutical and cognitive enhancement technologies may become not merely advantageous but essential for humanity to remain competitive, relevant, and adaptive in an AI-dominated landscape.
