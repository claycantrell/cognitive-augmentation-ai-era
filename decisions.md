## Scope & Framing
- **This is an argument paper, not a neutral survey.** The thesis has a clear position: cognitive enhancement is necessary for humanity to survive the AI era.
- **The argument has a specific arc:** history of mismanagement → existential stakes of AI → AI as cognitive sedative → enhancement as the solution → meditation as proof of concept → synthesis.
- **"Opium for the masses" is a central metaphor.** AI/ASI will sedate humanity the way smartphones/social media have, but worse. The parallel to smartphones is the key illustrative example.
- **Enhancement includes pharmacological AND technological** — nootropics, psychedelics, BCIs, neurofeedback. Not just "try harder" or "educate people."
- **The meditation parallel is important** — expert meditators are the proof that the cognitive states we need (empathy, metacognition, patience) are achievable. Enhancement is the delivery mechanism.
